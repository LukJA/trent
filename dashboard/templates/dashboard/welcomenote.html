Welcome to Fintrent.tech

FinTrent is dynamic web-app that allows you take control, and
understand your financial future.

Be sure to go over to the settings, and plug all your data into the system,
from there we will calculate and display key metrics about how your financial
situation looks set to develop, analysing your investments and assets over your entire
lifetime.

This was developped over the course of 24 hours, for the Hex Cambridge
2020 Hackathon, and is therefore pretty barebones and probably very broken.
Please post any issues you come accross on the GitHub repository!

With thanks from the developers- Matthew, Mounir, Luke and Kailen.
